About Us

The Prime Visa

The challenges and agony of bringing their loved ones to the United States have been the struggles for a long time by every petitioner and beneficiary in the US. The preparation of accurate documents alone, which is the first step to complying with the requirements of the US government, is always the hardest.

The visionary man, our founder, Mr. Norman Izon, a realtor who engaged in diversified business ventures, understands that immigration can be a frustrating process filled with legal jargon, paperwork, and uncertainty, and he felt the need to ease the burden and bridge the gap of the immigration journey.

To simplify the process and help you achieve your goals of international migration and fresh start-ups, our founder collaborated with Edmund Espeleta and his top-notch team in immigration consultancy with extensive knowledge and experience in all areas of immigration law and consultancy for decades and created The Prime Visa.

At The Prime Visa, we pride ourselves on our commitment to excellence. Our team stays updated on changes in immigration law and policy. We ensure that every client receives personalized attention and support throughout the entire process, and we will keep you informed every step of the way.
